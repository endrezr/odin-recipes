Pizza Recipe

Description:

This is a nice spaghetti recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup mushrooms
  • 1 cup tomatoes
  • 1 cup onion
  • 1 cup pepper
  • 1 cup bacon
  • 1 cup cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cut mushrooms into small pieces.
  3. Cut tomatoes into small pieces.
  4. Cut onion into small pieces.
  5. Cut pepper into small pieces.
  6. Cut bacon into small pieces.
  7. Cut cheese into small pieces.
  8. Place mushrooms on top of tomatoes.
  9. Place tomatoes on top of onion.
  10. Place onion on top of pepper.
  11. Place pepper on top of bacon.
  12. Place bacon on top of cheese.
  13. Place cheese on top of mushrooms.
